MultiplayerSettingsPacket (id: 139)

Purpose: Syncs multiplayer settings
Notes: This is used by EDU for joining players and removing players from your session, the settings (there is only one) is an enum for enabling/disabling/refreshing multiplayer join codes. Starts on the client, and a response to the client is issued from the server.
