SetActorMotionPacket (id: 40)

Purpose: This is used for the server to set the velocity of a client actor.
Notes: It is primarily relevant for client predicted entities like the player or a boat or horse they are in control of. For most other actor types it does nothing.
This is one of the packets that can directly affect player motion, for others, see:
- MovePlayerPacket
- CorrectPlayerMovePredictionPacket

Payload




Field NameField TypeField ConstraintsField Notes
Target Runtime ID ActorRuntimeID
Motion Vec3
Tick PlayerInputTick If this packet is referring to the player or a client predicted vehicle they are in control of, this should be the most recently processed PlayerInputTick from their PlayerAuthInputPacket. Otherwise zero.
