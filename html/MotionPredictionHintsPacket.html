MotionPredictionHintsPacket (id: 157)

Purpose: It is essentially a SetActionMotionPacket with a bool indicating if the actor was on the ground at the time the packet is sent or not.
Notes: This is from the server when spatial optimizations are enabled and the server does not send a spatial update.

Payload




Field NameField TypeField ConstraintsField Notes
mRuntimeId ActorRuntimeID Id of moving actor
mMotion Vec3 Position delta
mOnGround bool Not falling / jumping
